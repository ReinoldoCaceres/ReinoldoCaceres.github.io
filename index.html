Meet Dennis


Since I arrived in 1980, I have formed a special bond with the city of Azusa. It has become my home. Having attended college, met my wife, worked, worshipped and raised our kids here in the community, my roots have grown deep. I love the history and diversity of our city and have always believed that Azusa is a beautiful city with even greater potential.

Goals

01

Community Resources

Azusa has a wide variety of community resources available from a number of public & private groups and organizations. As a community, we need to coordinate and connect these groups and resources with the right people within our city who can benefit from them.

02

Support for Business

We all want a vibrant and diverse business community within our city. All businesses support a local workforce, shopping, entertainment opportunities and tax revenue for the city, benefiting all our residents. I will continue to support businesses to thrive and grow and I will work alongside the city staff to foster new development opportunities.

03

Thriving Neighborhoods

Azusa is increasingly becoming a collection of distinct neighborhoods, which often happens as cities grow. I will encourage local neighborhoods to foster and develop positive identities and spaces while strengthening our connectedness as one Azusa.
